What is Social Media

Social media are new media technologies that facilitate the creation, sharing and aggregation of content (such as ideas, interests, and other forms of expression) amongst virtual communities and networks
The term social in regard to media suggests platforms enable communal activity. Social media helps people connect and build networks.[6] Users access social media through web-based or mobile applications.
These interactive platforms allow individuals, communities, businesses, and organizations to share, co-create, discuss, participate in, and modify user-generated or self-curated content.
Social media is used to share memories, form friendships, and learn.[8] They may be used to promote people, companies, products, and ideas.[8] Social media can be used to consume, publish, or share news.
