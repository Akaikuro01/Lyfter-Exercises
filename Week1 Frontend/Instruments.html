Instrumentos Musicales

Music is an important form of expression that helps people communicate emotions, tell stories and connect with others. It is present in cultures all around the world and plays a role in celebrations, traditions and daily life.

Guitar

The guitar is a string instrument usually played with the fingers or a pick. It is extremely versatile and is used in many musical styles, from classical to rock and pop.

  • Type: String instrument
  • Main material: Wood (body and neck), metal or nylon strings
  • Common use: Solos, accompaniment and songwriting

Piano

The piano is a keyboard instrument where hammers strike strings inside the body. It can play both melody and harmony, which makes it one of the most complete and expressive instruments.

  • Type: String and percussion (hammered strings)
  • Main material: Wood, steel strings and felt-covered hammers
  • Common use: Solo performances, accompaniment and composition

Drum Set

The drum set is a percussion instrument made of several drums and cymbals. It provides rhythm and energy in bands and is essential in many modern music styles.

  • Type: Percussion instrument
  • Main material: Wood shells, metal hardware and plastic drumheads
  • Common use: Rhythm section in bands and live performances
